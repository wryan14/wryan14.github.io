"John Wesley's Sermon on the Transformation of Heart: Seeking Spiritual Rebirth and repentance"

"Take away the love of sinning, Almighty God, we pray; Give us a new heart beginning, A right spirit sealed this day."

Hymn Title: Hymn: "A New Heart" by John Wesley


John Wesley's sermon, "Circumcision of the Heart," emphasizes the need to seek renunciation of the world and a conversion experience. Wesley emphasizes that through faith and regeneration, people can become transformed and experience a spiritual rebirth and repentance. He explains that this transformation needs to stem from within, instead of being externally imposed and that it must be a personal, internal change. Wesley argues that this form of spiritual transformation is the only path to salvation. He goes on to discuss how faith transforms the individual, bringing the Holy Spirit and spiritual justification, thereby creating a clean heart. Finally, Wesley argues that the regeneration of the spirit is the highest good and a necessary component of pleasing God.
