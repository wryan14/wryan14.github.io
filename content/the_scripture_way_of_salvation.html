The Only Way to True Salvation: Exploring John Wesley's Sermon "The Scripture Way of Salvation"

"By grace are you saved, through faith; And that not of yourselves: 'Tis the gift of God, not of works, Lest any man should boast."

Hymn Title: Amazing Grace


John Wesley's sermon, The Scripture Way of Salvation, examines the steps people must take to ensure their salvation based on Biblical teachings. Wesley asserts that, through Scripture, people come to understand the necessity of repentance and faith; both of which are essential in experiencing true salvation. The sermon further highlights the role of the Holy Spirit in empowering individuals to repent and believe the Gospel of Christ. The sermon emphasizes that trusting in the Lord and His grace is the only way to achieve salvation. To teach these principles, Wesley uses multiple sound scriptural arguments to demonstrate that the Bible is the only true source of salvation. He calls listeners to faithfully adhere to the scripture and embrace its teachings if they intend to receive salvation.
