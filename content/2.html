Experience True Spiritual Transformation with John Wesley's "Circumcision of the Heart" Sermon!

" "Ye must be born again; 'tis the boundary line, New Nature's given, in your hearts it must shine; On you the Spirit from above must descend; Oh, 'tis an altar that must never end!" "

Hymn Title: "Hymn Title: "Circumcision of the Heart""


John Wesley's sermon, Circumcision of the Heart, stresses the importance of a spiritual transformation with a newfound faith in God. He argues that many individuals attempt to internalize Christian values and doctrines without actually engaging in true spiritual transformation. He equates this shallow understanding of faith with being "uncircumcised in heart," while true faith is likened to the concept of "circumcision of the heart," wherein individuals have a genuine and sincere connection to the divine. He emphasizes that this transformation must not only be outwardly expressed, but also internally felt, making the spiritual experience a fundamental act. Ultimately, Wesley argues that individuals must truly engage in a spiritual transformation in order to achieve redemption and salvation.
