"Finding Grace Through the 'Circumcision of the Heart': Exploring John Wesley's Sermon"

O blessed Lord, You drew my heart To trust Your grace from off the tree; My life with You I did impart Through Your circumcision made for me.

Hymn Title: Hymn: "At Calvary"


John Wesley's sermon "Circumcision of the Heart" focuses on how true obedience to God can only be achieved by the transformation of one's heart from within. He argues that it is not enough to follow the exterior law of the Old Testament, as true righteousness is something that comes from the soul, from spiritual obsession and from being connected with God. He also discussed how this is something that every Christian can attain and must strive for. He says that it is only through this spiritual transformation that we can receive salvation and find God's grace. He thus encourages all readers to practice circumcision of the heart, through faith and devotion, in order to fulfill their true communication with the divine.
