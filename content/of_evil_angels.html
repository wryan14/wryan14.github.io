"Battling Spiritual Enemies: Examining John Wesley's Sermon 'Of Evil Angels'"

"What no eye has seen, nor ear has heard, nor thought of human heart, all that from heaven thy mercy gives, Jesus thy gospel imparts."

Hymn Title: Lo! What a Glorious Sight Appears


John Wesley's sermon Of Evil Angels examines the consequences of Christian disobedience as a result of spiritual influences, while emphasizing that God still reigns supreme. Wesley discusses the spiritual conflict between good and evil forces, and notes that a lack of vigilance and obedience towards God can open the door for sin to enter and lead astray. He emphasizes the role of angels in this conflict, both in the temptations that come from evil and in the strength they can provide to face them. Ultimately, Wesley contends that though humans may be up against powerful spiritual forces, ultimately, God reigns supreme and will protect and provide for those who trust in Him.
